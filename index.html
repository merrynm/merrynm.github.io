H e l l o !

My name is Merryn and I'm a May 2020 graduate from Michigan State University with a BS in Computer Science and a concentration in Neuroscience.

Why the Neuroscience concentration, you may ask? Well, I actually spent my first ~4 years of undergrad studing Neuroscience in anticipation of attending medical school. I decided (very late) that it wasn't the path for me, took an introductory programming course on a whim and loved it so much that I decided to change my degree to CS and pursue it full time. I then spent the rest of college cramming in an entirely new 4-year degree into just 2 years. While I will always love Neuroscience, and would love to apply it to my career somehow, programming (and all of the problem-solving that comes with it) is what makes me happiest.

Finishing my last semester and graduating during the COVID-19 Pandemic has certainly had its unique challenges, along with already having a late start to my degree, but I am eager to begin my career in this field and see what the world has in store for me.
