My name is Merryn and I'm a May 2020 graduate from Michigan State University with a BS in Computer Science and a concentration in Neuroscience.

I have spent the last 3 years working at Stellantis, the fourth-largest automaker in the world.

I initially interviewed here for an open-ended product development role. After hire I was placed on Stellantis' in-house software development team for in-vehicle apps after speaking of my passion for and achievements within Android app development during my time at Michigan State. I am so grateful for the team I was placed on, because it only furthered my love for Android development and made me want to pursue a career within it in the forseeable future.
